Food Guide

Where to Eat Near Toffee Homestay

By Toffee Homestay · 19 June 2026 · 5 min read

← Back to guide book

One of the best things about staying in Ninh Bình is the food — and you don't have to go far. Here are our favourite local restaurants and street-food spots near the homestay, from regional specialities to late-night snacks. Distances are measured from Toffee Homestay.

Tip: addresses are written the Vietnamese way. The easiest thing to do is copy the name into Google Maps, or just show this page to a Zalo message and we'll point you the right way.

Ninh Bình specialities

If it's your first time here, start with the dishes the region is famous for — goat meat (dê núi), crispy scorched rice (cơm cháy) and eel.

Nhà Hàng Đức Dê — goat hotpot & grills

The classic spot for Ninh Bình goat meat, done many ways. Worth the short ride.
📍 Số 29, ngõ 458 Nguyễn Huệ, Nam Bình · ~4.2 km · View on map →

Nhà Hàng Cơm Niêu Việt Xưa — clay-pot rice

Traditional clay-pot rice and home-style dishes, right by Kỳ Lân lake.
📍 80 Đường Tràng An, Tân Thành · ~1.5 km (opposite Kỳ Lân lake) · View on map →

Cơm Đốt Thố Trạng Nguyên — baked pot rice

Another great choice for the famous baked rice pot.
📍 183 Đường Lương Văn Thăng, Đông Thành · ~2.5 km · View on map →

Miến lươn Thảo Linh — eel glass noodles

Local favourite for eel vermicelli, right by the Hoa Lư Ancient Town area.
📍 16-18 Đường Tràng An, Tân Thành · ~1.5 km · View on map →

Nhà hàng Vũ Bảo — freshwater fish & specialities

Known for cá nhệch (a regional fish dish) and other local specialities.
📍 Số 37 Nguyễn Huệ, Nam Bình · ~4.5 km · View on map →

Rice, noodles & comfort food

Bún chả Tình Mai

Grilled pork with vermicelli — a Vietnamese lunch classic.
📍 07 Phạm Hồng Thái, Vân Giang · View on map →

Bún mọc Tố Như

Comforting pork-and-mushroom ball noodle soup.
📍 74 Lê Hồng Phong, Đông Thành · ~2.7 km · View on map →

Cơm tấm sườn nướng Út Thương

Broken rice with grilled pork ribs — hearty and satisfying.
📍 45 Đinh Tiên Hoàng, Đông Thành · ~3 km · View on map →

Bánh cuốn chả nóng bà Liên

Steamed rice rolls served hot with grilled pork — a great breakfast.
📍 15 Võ Thị Sáu, Đông Thành · ~2.5 km · View on map →

Street food & snacks

For something lighter, a late-night bite, or dessert.

Chân gà nướng & Ăn vặt Phố 8

Grilled chicken feet and a spread of Vietnamese snacks.
📍 Khu Phố 8, Phường Vân Giang · ~2.2 km · View on map →

Bánh tráng trộn & Ăn vặt Bống Bống

Rice-paper salad and street snacks — fun and cheap.
📍 62 Lương Văn Tụy, Tân Thành · ~2 km · View on map →

Bánh mỳ cay Hải Phòng chính gốc

Spicy Hải Phòng-style mini baguettes.
📍 52 Đinh Tất Miễn, Tân Thành · ~1.8 km · View on map →

Ốc luộc mắm gừng bà Hoàng

Boiled snails with ginger fish sauce — a beloved evening snack.
📍 Ngõ 2 Tôn Đức Thắng, Đông Thành · ~2.8 km · View on map →

Chợ Quê Quán

Countryside-style eatery with a range of local dishes.
📍 Tràng An 2, Tân Trung, Tân Thành · ~1.8 km · View on map →

Chè Bưởi & Kem Xôi Huế — dessert

Finish with sweet pomelo chè and Huế-style sticky-rice ice cream.
📍 14 ngõ 10 Tràng An, Tân Thành · ~1.4 km · View on map →
