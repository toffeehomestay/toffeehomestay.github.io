About Toffee Homestay

A home with a story, in the heart of the heritage

Built in 1999, this house is part of our family's history — raised by our grandfather as a gift of love and now lovingly restored by his grandchildren. We didn't just build a place to stay; we preserved a heart. Step in, make yourself at home, and write your own chapter here.

Green, quiet and beautifully central, Toffee Homestay blends classic charm with modern comfort. You're moments from local eateries, shops and a pharmacy, with a lake and leafy park about a kilometre away.
